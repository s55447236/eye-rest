# Privacy Policy for Eye Rest Cat

**Last Updated:** November 21, 2025

## Introduction
"Eye Rest Cat" ("we", "us", or "our") is a Chrome extension designed to help users rest their eyes periodically. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains that we do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect any personal information.**
*   We do not track your browsing history.
*   We do not collect your IP address or location.
*   We do not collect any usage data or analytics.
*   We do not have any servers that receive data from the extension.

## Permissions Usage
The extension requires specific permissions to function, but these are used solely for the core functionality on your local device:
*   **`alarms`**: Used to schedule the timer (e.g., every 60 minutes) to remind you to rest.
*   **`scripting` / `activeTab` / `Host Permissions`**: Used only to display the "Rest Overlay" and the animated cat on top of the web page you are currently viewing. The extension does not read or modify the content of the websites you visit, except to overlay the reminder.

## Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
